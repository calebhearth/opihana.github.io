Data Visualization Tool

Contrast 2017

I worked on wireframes and mocks for this private client. His goal was to find a better way to gather and display salary data with more critical information--from gender to title to years of experience. We decided to incentivize people to provide more information by providing them with an updated graph every time they added information to the tool. It's informative and, as an animation, entertaining.

Future ideas for the tool include saving user's information over time so that they can track their own career progress over time and visually identify what changes caused their increases in salary and benefits.
